  • Expression Capacity

    • LncExpDB estimates lncRNAs' expression capacities based on their expression levels. For a certain context, HC lncRNAs are expressed at high expression levels in at least one condition, while LC lncRNAs are expressed at low expression levels in all conditions. The remaining lncRNAs are MC.

    HC: High-Capacity; MC: Medium-Capacity; LC: Low-Capacity; NE: Not Expressed. See more about expression capacity
